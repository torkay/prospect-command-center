FAQ

Is this a contact database?

No. LeadSwarm is a discovery + qualification engine. It helps you find prospects and prioritize outreach based on observable signals.

What does “Start trial” mean?

Create an account and you can run searches in the app. Usage limits depend on your plan and configuration.

Can I use this outside Australia?

The workflow is designed for English-language markets and local-services style searches. Availability depends on configured providers.
